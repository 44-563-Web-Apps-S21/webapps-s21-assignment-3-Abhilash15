Cook Egg dosa :-

  1. take dosa powder and water.
  2. grind both make a dosa mix.
  3. take 2 eggs yawk in glass.
  4. put mix in pan in round shape.
    1. add egg mix on top of dosa.
    2. add oil on top.
  5. flip the dosa.
  6. dosa is ready.

Ingredients :-

  • dosa powder.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 1 table spoon oil.
  • salt and pepper.
